Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

SkinAI ("we", "us", or "our") provides cosmetic skin analysis and skincare routine suggestions. This Privacy Policy explains what information we process, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Process

Scan photos. SkinAI lets you take or select a face scan photo. Before subscription, your basic scan experience is handled on your device and the full cloud AI report is locked. If you subscribe and confirm AI report generation, a compressed version of your scan photo may be securely sent to our AI service to generate your cosmetic skin report, skin score, visible skin insights, and skincare plan.

AI skin report data. The AI service may return cosmetic estimates such as skin age, overall score, visible radiance, pores, fine lines, even tone, hydration, and a suggested skincare plan. These are cosmetic insights only and are not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Subscription information. Purchases and subscriptions are handled by Apple and RevenueCat. We do not receive your payment card details. We may receive subscription status, product identifier, renewal state, and anonymous purchase identifiers so the app can unlock paid features.

Product interaction data. We may collect app interaction events, such as onboarding progress, scan completion, paywall views, subscription events, and routine completion. We use this information to improve the app, understand feature performance, and measure marketing campaigns. We do not send your scan photo, skin age, or skin scores to advertising attribution services.

Device and diagnostic information. We may process basic technical information such as app version, device type, crash logs, and performance data to maintain and improve the app.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide face scan, cosmetic skin analysis, AI report generation, and skincare plan features.
  • To unlock subscription features and restore purchases.
  • To improve app reliability, user experience, and product performance.
  • To measure advertising and subscription funnel performance without using scan photos for ad tracking.
  • To prevent abuse, troubleshoot issues, and maintain security.

3. AI Processing

When you choose to generate a full AI report after subscribing, your compressed scan photo is sent over an encrypted connection to our backend and AI processing provider. The AI provider analyzes visible cosmetic skin cues and returns a structured report for the app.

SkinAI does not use AI analysis to identify you as a person. The app does not provide medical diagnosis, detect disease, or replace advice from a qualified healthcare professional.

4. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use scan photos for advertising tracking.
  • We do not send your scan photos to Tenjin, Mixpanel, or advertising networks.
  • We do not claim to provide medical diagnosis or treatment.
  • We do not require an account to use the app.

5. Service Providers

We use trusted service providers to operate SkinAI:

  • Apple for App Store purchases and subscriptions.
  • RevenueCat for subscription management and entitlement status.
  • Cloudflare for backend hosting and secure API routing.
  • AI processing provider for generating cosmetic skin reports from scan photos.
  • Analytics and attribution providers, such as Mixpanel and Tenjin, for product analytics and campaign measurement. Scan photos and AI skin scores are not sent to these providers.

6. Data Retention

Scan photos may be stored locally on your device so the app can display your latest scan and progress experience. You can remove this data by deleting the app.

When a scan photo is sent to our backend for AI report generation, it is used to generate the report and is not intended to be used to identify you or for advertising. We aim to minimize retention of uploaded scan photos and related AI request data, keeping it only as long as reasonably necessary to provide the feature, maintain security, troubleshoot errors, or comply with legal obligations.

7. Your Choices

  • You can choose not to generate a cloud AI report.
  • You can deny camera or photo library access in iOS Settings.
  • You can cancel your subscription through your Apple ID subscription settings.
  • You can delete locally stored app data by deleting the app from your device.
  • You may contact us to request assistance with privacy-related questions.

8. Children

SkinAI is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect information processed by the app, including encrypted connections for data sent to our backend. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your information and minimize unnecessary data processing.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and may provide additional notice where appropriate.
